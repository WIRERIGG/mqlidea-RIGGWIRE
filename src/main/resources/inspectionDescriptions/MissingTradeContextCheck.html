Reports a trade operation (OrderSend(), OrderClose(), OrderModify(), OrderDelete()) performed without a trade-context guard (MQL4).

In MQL4 the trade context is single-threaded: if another expert advisor or a manual action is already trading, the operation fails with error 146 (trade context busy). Check IsTradeAllowed() or IsTradeContextBusy() before trading, and wait or retry when the context is busy.
